RandFourBoost

RandFourBoost is a hybrid machine learning model that combines Random Forest, XGBoost, and Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) to predict the genre of music tracks and improve classification accuracy for beginner music producers.

What problem it Solves

RandFourBoost helps beginner music producers identify whether their music aligns with the intended genre, providing guidance on genre-specific characteristics to stay on track.

Technologies Used

Python with scikit-learn, planned to be deployed with Flask for a web-based interface.
